Sahaba Youth Centre, Incoming Hub for the Youth

We're excited to announce that the Sahaba Youth Centre (SYC) will officially open on October 1st, 2024! This new facility will serve as a vibrant hub for the community's youth, offering Quran and Arabic classes, a basketball court, a lounge area, and a dedicated homework club.

NEW Al Faruq Islamic School

By the Grace of Allah SWT, The Al Faruq Islamic Centre Project and the community have reached a new milestone, we have been fully approved by Alberta Education. We will be accepting KG & Grade 1 students only, limited spots for September 2024.
